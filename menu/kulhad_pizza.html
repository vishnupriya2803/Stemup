Kulhad Pizza

Experience the fusion of tradition and innovation with our Kulhad Pizza.

Baked in a traditional clay pot, this pizza boasts a smoky aroma and a unique earthy flavor.

The crust is crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, providing the perfect base for a rich tomato sauce, melted cheese, and a selection of fresh toppings.

Served in its own kulhad (clay cup), it offers a rustic and memorable dining experience.

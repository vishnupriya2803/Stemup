Veg Burger

The Veg Burger is a wholesome creation featuring a patty made from a blend of fresh vegetables, legumes, and grains, seasoned with aromatic spices.

Grilled to perfection, the patty is complemented by crisp lettuce, ripe tomatoes, and a dollop of creamy mayonnaise, all sandwiched between a soft, toasted bun.

This burger offers a satisfying and flavorful option for vegetarians and health-conscious diners alike.
